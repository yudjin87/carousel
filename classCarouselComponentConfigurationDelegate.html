Detailed Description

Default implementation of the IComponentConfigurationDelegate for configuring/deconfiguring the started components.

It also manages what exactly have been changed in GUI during component's configuraion - it is important for the further deconfiguring and also could be usefull for displaying which operations have been registered by components.
